How It Works

  • ♦This Minecraft Roulette has a total of 22 fields: 10 black, 10 red, and 2 green spaces. Players place their bets on colors before the wheel spins.
  • ♦When the wheel stops, the Diamond Block lands on one of these fields. If it lands on your chosen color, you win according to the payout rates.
  • ♦The two green fields are rarer but offer higher rewards, adding extra excitement and risk to the game.
  • ♦Overall, this setup keeps the classic roulette experience but with a unique Minecraft twist.

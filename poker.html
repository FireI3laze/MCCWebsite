Bets

After each round the players enter a bet phase. The player left to the dealer starts with any bet (in round 1 every player has to bet at least 1 diamond). Rotating clockwise, all subsequent players must consider if they call, raise or fold.

If a player calls, that means that they match the current highest bet.

If a player raises, then they increased the bet and everyone has to at least call or fold.

If a player folds, they quit that round, lose any chance of winning and all bets they have done.

This continues until all bet an equal amount or everyone except one folds.

The winner of a game receives all bets. Player can agree on bet limits.

How It Works

  • ♦In this version of Poker, every player receives 2 hand cards and up to 3 cards will be put on the table.
  • ♦Each player tries to get the strongest card constellation by combining their hand cards with table cards.
  • ♦Upon obtaining hand cards and after each card put on the table the players enter a bet phase.
  • ♦Bets are put in the chest in the center of the table. Each row of the chest has a named paper, representing the rounds.
  • ♦After the last betting phase (4 in total), all players that are still in the game reveal their cards so the winner can be determined.
  • ♦Finally every player puts their cards in the chest next to them and the dealer wipes the table. Then a new round can be started.

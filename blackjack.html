How It Works

In this version of Black Jack, the player tries to get as close as possible to a maximum (14) score by drawing cards valued from 1 to 7, without going over that maximum (15). The player always draws first and can take as many cards as they want to improve their total score.

After the player finishes, the dealer takes their turn, drawing cards with values from 1 to 7 to try and beat the player’s score without exceeding the maximum.

If the player exceeds the maximum score, they lose immediately.

At the end, the side with the score closest to the maximum without going over wins. If both have the same score, the result is a tie.

Common Issues & Solutions

Score

I can't score

The score always resets to 0 immediately after a card was drawn

Solution:

This can happen if, unfortunately, a new game was started while a backup has been performed. Just inform me asap (@Fire_I3laze in Discord or pn me).

Score was incorrectly raised

After I submitted my score, the machine went playing and suddenly increased my score, causing me to lose

Solution:

This only happens if you (or anyone else) accidentally pressed the "submit button" a second time. Then the machine rolls back to the player score and adds the pulled numbers there. If that happens, it is honestly your own fault because it is stated in the rules to only press the button once. But if you report it to me ingame while I am online, I'll mostly pay your bets back, yet your possibly good score will be lost

Machine Start

Game doesn't start

When I press the right button to start a new game, the machine refuses to start and the interface stays at the previous game states

Solution:

Just wait. The machine is still paying out the bets from the previous round. You likely lost to a high bet. If you pay attention you'll likely hear the clock and dropper working. The next round can be started as soon as the noise has been cancelled

Machine freezes after start

When I press the right button to start a new game, the interface lights switch off and stay that way

Solution:

Just wait. You've likely put in a huge bet and the machine is counting. Due to limited Minecraft physics, this will take a noticeable time from ~32 Diamonds on. You can always check the bet barrel to see the progress. Once all diamonds are gone, the machine will pull the first card for you and you can go on.

Payout

Wrong / No Payout

The machine does not pay out correctly or not at all

Solution:

First check whether the device is actually displaying your expected score. If that is the case, make sure that you have the in mind. If that is also the case and you are very sure the output is not matching your input * ratio, either contact me (@Fire_I3laze in Discord or pn me) or feel free to check the Diamond bet stock yourself if you know where it is. If the chest is not empty, I am afraid you are wrong and the machine did pay out correctly. But depending on the amount, I might be open to settle the discrepancy.
